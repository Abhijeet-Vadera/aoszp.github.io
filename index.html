Zirconium Project

We are focusing on Stability. We are bringing more Stable ROM for your devices and it got everything that will make you stick to it. We are looking forword for precious support of you all lovely USERS.

Features

Zirconium got everything that will make you stick to it

Stability

This is our prime focus, we wanted to make a rom that remains stable and userfriendly in long run.

Simplicity

We keep it simple and mostly near to stock AOSP, which increases performance.

Minimalistic

We won't be adding tons of features like few other roms have, in our opinion most of those features are never used.

Customizability

There are still customizations that are mostly used by everybody.
